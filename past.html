My Past

By Jeff Manville - 7/3/2018

I grew up on a farm in Southbury CT. I'be always had a talent for math and software. I spent my summers in New Hampshire or working on the farm, moving thousands of pounds of hay for our cows. In High school, I rowed, acted, sang, and found ever new inventive ways to break the rules

Then, I went to Northeastern University for Industrial Engineering. I loved the mathematics and the focus on efficiency. Not just short term, but the efficiency in a comprehensive way covering the human and machine aspects. Northeastern gave me the chance to study and work in manufacturing, healthcare, and the finance industry. I graduated in Last May and then I spent a few months in Puerto Rico. I got out before the hurricanes and went back to the family farm. Then, I got a job at HBS and now I'm here.
